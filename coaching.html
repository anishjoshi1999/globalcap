What we’re offering

We conduct various types of tests for your recruitment.

Citizenship Test

Citizenship Test

A citizenship test is an exam that assesses an individual's knowledge of the country's history, laws, and culture to determine their eligibility for citizenship.

Toefl

Toefl

"TOEFL" which stands for Test of English as a Foreign Language. It is a standardized test used to evaluate the English proficiency of non-native speakers who want to enroll in English-speaking universities or other academic institutions.

Take Ielts

Take Ielts

"IELTS" which stands for International English Language Testing System. It is a standardized test that assesses the language proficiency of non-native English speakers who need to study, work, or immigrate in English-speaking countries. The test measures proficiency in four areas: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.

PTE Coaching

PTE Coaching

PTE coaching is training for the computer-based Pearson Test of English, which measures language proficiency for academic, immigration, and professional purposes. Coaching includes practice tests, feedback, and strategies to improve test-taking skills.

Skills Exam

Skills Exam

A skills exam is an assessment used to evaluate a person's proficiency in a particular skill or set of skills by measuring their ability to perform specific tasks related to the skill. Results can be used to determine suitability for a job or identify areas for further training.

Management Test

Management Test

Management tests are assessments designed to evaluate a person's knowledge, skills, and abilities related to management. These tests can be used for various purposes, such as employee selection, career development, and training needs assessment.
